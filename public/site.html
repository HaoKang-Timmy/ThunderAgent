WHY THUNDERAGENT?

FAST

ThunderAgent improves agentic serving throughputs by 1.5-3.6x compared to state-of-the-art inference systems. Our program-aware scheduler eliminates KV-cache thrashing during tool execution and resolves cross-node memory imbalances through dynamic migration.

> KV cache hit rate near 100%
> State-aware pause & resume
> Up to 3.9x RL rollout speedup

SIMPLE

Integrates with just 2 lines of code through an OpenAI-compatible API. ThunderAgent wraps existing LLM engines and tool orchestrators, decoupling scheduling from execution backends like vLLM or SGLang.

> OpenAI-compatible endpoints
> Drop-in replacement
> Works with vLLM & SGLang

ROBUST

Scales seamlessly across multiple nodes and diverse agentic workflows. The lifecycle-aware garbage collector prevents resource leakage while asynchronous environment preparation overlaps I/O with LLM reasoning.

> Multi-node scaling
> Lifecycle-aware GC
> 4.2x disk memory savings
